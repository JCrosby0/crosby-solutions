About me

I love solutions.

I like data, logic, bringing order to chaos, automation. I love discovering and consuming modern technology in the process.

As an engineer, I delivered data acquisition solutions:
  • communicated with and designed solutions to satisfy multiple interested parties
  • planned and executed data acquisition programmes
  • acquired formation data to evaluate oil and gas reservoirs.

As a software end-user, I used a python-based platform to collate, visualise and interpret my data. I augmented that software with customised workflows to improve its performance, streamline data loading and automate repetitive tasks. Some of my enhancements were noticed by the developers, and I was invited to present on multiple occaissions in their offices, and at their international conference.

As a web-developer, I built the front-end for a data storage, visualisation and computation platform (similar to above) in VueJS, complete with:
  • A full suite of scratch-built D3.js custom visualisation plots and charts
  • Websocket data streaming and local client data storage, optimised for low-bandwidth performance
  • Full CRUD solutions for multiple data types, with customisable user permissions
